Action Plan by Quadrant

Procurement Strategy

Recommended procurement strategies based on Kraljic Matrix segmentation:

Strategic Items

  • Develop partnerships with key suppliers
  • Sign long-term contracts with quality/volume agreements
  • Conduct joint business planning (forecasting, innovation)
  • Dual-source if possible (e.g., for peanuts)
  • Examples: Buffalo Milk, EV Olive Oil, Specialty Peanuts, Artisanal Flour

Leverage Items

  • Aggressively negotiate with suppliers
  • Use volume consolidation and annual tenders
  • Implement reverse auctions for best pricing
  • Switch suppliers easily to get best Total Cost of Ownership
  • Examples: Rolled Oats, Palm Oil (with certification), Plastic Packaging

Bottleneck Items

  • Mitigate risk through long-term contracts
  • Maintain safety stock where possible
  • Explore technical alternatives (e.g., different culture blends)
  • Simplify specifications if feasible
  • Examples: Probiotic Cultures, Pure Honey

Non-Critical Items

  • Reduce administrative costs through automation
  • Use procurement catalogs and P-cards
  • Outsource to a distributor for consolidated ordering
  • Implement automated ordering systems
  • Examples: Salt, Yeast, Standard Corrugated Boxes
